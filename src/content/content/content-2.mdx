## Capturing Stunning Landscapes

Landscape photography is all about capturing the beauty of nature. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. **Golden Hour**: Shoot during sunrise or sunset for soft, warm lighting.
2. **Foreground Interest**: Include interesting elements in the foreground to add depth.
3. **Wide-Angle Lens**: Use a wide-angle lens to capture expansive views.
